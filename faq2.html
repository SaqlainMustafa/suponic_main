Is wagering legal?

Wagering is not exactly like betting, which relies heavily on pure luck, but it relies mostly on the skills to defeat the opponent player, the fruit of effort in endless training. Gambling and Wagering are fundamentally different. Gambling is relying on a pure chance, betting everything on a luck.
